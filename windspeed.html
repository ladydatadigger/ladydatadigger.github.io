Wind Speed


When we plot latitude vs. wind speed we don't see a strong relationship between the two. Cities across the Earth show minimal wind speed, i.e, 0-10 mph winds are the normal during this date in January. High winds (25 mph or greater) are less frequent but they are in cities between 50-70 latitude.
